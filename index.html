Naval Architect

Rokibul Hasan is a renowned naval architect from Bangladesh. He has been involved in the construction and design of various naval ships over the years. His passion for maritime engineering and design has led him to become a leading figure in the field.

About Me

Ship Design & Parametric Modeling

Rokibul has developed expertise in parametric modeling using tools like Grasshopper, focusing on ship rudder design. He has created a script that automates the design process by accepting input parameters such as NACA number, height, breadth, and plate thickness to generate a 3D rudder model. This script is part of his ongoing work to incorporate Classification Society rules into the design process, aiming to streamline the industry standard.

Extracurricular Involvement

Graphic Design: A skilled graphic designer, Rokibul is involved with the MIST Innovation Club and MIST Robotics Club, contributing to several design projects. His work has included designing for publications such as the SAIL 2022 magazine edition. His expertise in Adobe Photoshop has made him a valuable team member for various creative endeavors.
